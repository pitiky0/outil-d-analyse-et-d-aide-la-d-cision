Change Engineer Formation Program

Welcome to the Change Engineer Formation Program! This program is designed to equip change engineers with the skills and knowledge necessary to effectively manage and implement changes within our organization. Below, you'll find an overview of the program curriculum along with links to resources for each module.

Program Overview:

  1. Introduction to Change Management:

    Gain an understanding of the principles and concepts of change management. Learn about the importance of effective change management in organizational success.

    Download PDF - Introduction to Change Management

  2. Change Process and Methodologies:

    Explore different change management methodologies and models. Understand the change process from initiation to implementation and evaluation.

    View Online - Change Process and Methodologies

  3. Stakeholder Analysis and Engagement:

    Learn techniques for identifying and analyzing stakeholders. Understand strategies for engaging and communicating with stakeholders throughout the change process.

    Watch Video - Stakeholder Analysis and Engagement
